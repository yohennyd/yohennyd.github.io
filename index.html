Brief Summary

Many of you might be familiar with this popular sitcom. It is a show based in the
late 90s and early 2000s. It focuses on the lives of 6 friends, set in NYC and how they
navigate through breakups and life changes altogether.

Characters

Monica Geller

She is high maintance and obssesed with cleaning and having everything organized.
She loves being the host and is Ross' sister.

Ross Geller

Monica's brother and studies paleontology. He's been divorced 3 times and has a son.
He's been in love with Rachel since high school and they both navigate their on-and-off
relationship throughout the show.

Rachel Green

She is a push over and moved to the city to start a new life-away from her wealthy
family. Her friends help her adapt and she learns about independence.
She also loves fashion.

Phoebe Buffay

She is very spontaneous and unique. She loves to sing and play thre guitar
and friendship means a lot to her. She had a rough childhood and is the epitome of
resilience and versatility.

Joey Tribbiani

He is funny and a little slow. He is the best of the friends. He loves sandwiches and pizza.
And he's also a Knicks fan.

Chandler Bing

He uses sarcasm and jokes as a coping mechanism because of the rough childhood he had.
He is afraid of commitment but finds the love of his life later on in the show.

Why I like the show

I always watch Friends whenever I have a chance. Even though it first aired more than
20 years ago, they still put it on live TV. It is a comfort show. It's good to watch random
episode. It is those type of shows that you can watch over and over again and
not get bored.

Why you should watch it

It teaches the true meaning of friendships despite the harships and betrayals.
You can watch it whenever you want to smile, cry or laugh.
You learn how to forgive, get past things, adapt and accept others for who
are.
